Ticket Roll Line Renderings

During the Spring of 2016 I was running the Carnegie Mellon movie theatre on weekends, where we sold tickets from tightly packed rolls. I was also hanging out at the STUDIO for creative Inquiry where saw the amazing Ben Snell make some drawings with a robot arm that reminded me of the patterns that I was observing in the ticket rolls. So, I decided to go and make some simple 2D computer generated line drawings representing my own take on the phenomena. I dedicate them to my friend Ben. Please take note of the aliasing patterns and the distictions between line size, line curvature, etc.
