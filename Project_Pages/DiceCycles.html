Dice Cycles

Dice Cycles is a Game that I invented back in 2012 based on the light cycles game in the Tron movies and my interest in creating random boards through dice. This is a toy program that provides the representational capabilities to play a game of DiceCycles. Download.

Rules

  • A 10 by 10 board is randomly generated by putting randomly oriented dice at every location.
  • Both players choose distinct starting location by marking one of the locations. This location becomes their current location.
  • Every turn, players must make a number of moves equal to the number on the dice of the current location at the beginning of the turn.
  • -- Moves must be 1 square up, left, right, or down from their current location. Once moved, the player marks the location and it becomes their current location.
  • -- Players may not move to locations that have already been marked.
  • -- Players may not move out of the bounds of the board.
  • Turns procceed in clockwise order.
  • A player loses if they cannot make a valid move.
