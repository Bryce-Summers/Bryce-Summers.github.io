People

Names Why I like these people.
Golan LevinGolan is a New Media Artist and professor who cares about people. He is very good at providing high quality critiscism and advice. He routinely works to provide support for more students to do more creative projects.
Keenan CraneKeenan is a Discrete Differential Geometer who is able to concentrate on the things that matter, including his personal ideals, even amidst all of the noise that comes his way. I am a fan of his visual style and approach to teaching and communicating. I also like that he cares about mathematics proper.
Helen WangHelen is a Collegiate Student Life Professional who is adept at seeing through the shy adaptor layers of students to the things they care about and the potential they have inside.
Ben SnellBen is an Experiental Artist who makes work that grafts design sensibilities onto structural forms I found that his polar etch-a-sketch and robot drawings resonated with me. I dedicated the ticket roll renderings to Ben.
Muhammad Ali Muhammad Ali was a well known Boxer in the 20th century. I love his can do attitude and that he believed in himself first, then actualized what he believed. I also admire his dedication to his personal ideals.
Robert Tarjan Tarjan is a Computer Scientist who has invented quite a few data structures and surprisingly efficient algorithms. He wrote the book "Data Structures and Network Algorithms", which is written in a concise, yet clear manner. If you share my notion of fun, you should go and read it.
Danny SleatorDanny Sleator is a Computer Scientist who worked with Tarjan as a Ph.D. student and partially invented the concept of Amortized Analysis, Competitive Analysis in addition to several data structures such as splay trees, Link/Cut Trees, and Skew heaps.
Walt DisneyWalt Disney was a futurist working in the 20th century. He cared about technical quality in his work and had a penchant for new forms of transportation and urban planning.
