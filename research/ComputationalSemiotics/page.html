Computational Semiotics

by Bryce Summers

This page presents my work on computational semiotics theories for human communication in addition to tools putting these theories into practice. The tools focus on projecting the meanings onto a plane, which is the limit of what our eyes can perceive at once.

An idea for structuring stuff: Geometry is created, then it branches out into object appearance, movement, and relationships to the environment of objects around it.

All of the algorithms will be synthesized into one online image creation tool.
