Thesis

by Bryce Summers

I am building theories, methedologies for applying these theories, and tools exemplifying these methedologies.

I disagree that research should be shared in writing. I think that writing is good for peer to peer communication, but not as good for communicating to people without a corpus of understanding in the topic. I also think that people need to apply the knowledge before they can really understand it.
